## Suite 6 Detail

`test_ga_reliability_50` runs 50 seeds x 4 stages (200 runs) with a Clopper-Pearson CI lower-bound check (≥0.929). At the default 600s timeout, it reaches seed ~4-5 before timing out. With `GA_RELIABILITY_SEEDS=5` (20 runs), all 20/20 converge but the CI lower bound (0.832) is mathematically below the threshold due to small sample size. This is a runtime budget issue, not a correctness regression. The 50-seed budget optimization is tracked as a post-beta deferred item.
